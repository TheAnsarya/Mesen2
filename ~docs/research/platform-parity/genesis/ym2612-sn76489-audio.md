# Genesis YM2612 and SN76489 Audio

## Scope

Audio correctness depends on chip timing and synchronization with both CPUs.

## Findings

- Genesis Plus GX offers multiple YM core paths with explicit timing ratio handling.
- PicoDrive implements YM2612 with state-focused persistence and timing integration points.
- MAME ties YM and PSG behavior into machine-level hardware models.
